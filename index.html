About me


I am a fashion designer with a rich history of more than 15 years in the fashion industry. Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to develop my own business, consolidating my unique and creative vision in the world of design. During the course of the pandemic, my professional path took a significant turn when I discovered 3D digital fashion. This meeting not only represented a challenge in difficult times, but also ignited in me a new passion for innovation and creativity in clothing design. My fascination with digital fashion led me to not only embrace new technologies, but also to constantly look for ways to improve and expand my skills. Among the programs I have learned are Clo 3D, Marvelous Designer, Style3D, ZBrush and Blender. These tools have allowed me to take my designs to a three-dimensional level, exploring new frontiers and creating unique and innovative pieces.Contenido general.
